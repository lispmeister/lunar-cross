## Phase 1: Corrections to DESIGN-AND-CALCULATIONS.md

All changes cascade from two root corrections: (a) SHG stage required for 532 nm, lowering wall-plug from 35% to 30%; and (b) IMM-α efficiency is 32% BOL, not 33%.

### §3.1 Laser — SHG clarification

Old text described the laser as "30–40% wall-plug, 8–15 kW electrical." This was wrong because IPG YLS series is 1070 nm IR; reaching 532 nm requires a second-harmonic generation (SHG) crystal stage.

| Parameter | Old | New |
|---|---|---|
| Wall-plug efficiency | 30–40% | 25–35% (baseline 30%, including SHG) |
| Electrical power | 8–15 kW | 12–20 kW |
| Mass | ~150–250 kg | ~200–300 kg (including SHG crystal assembly) |
| Vendor note | IPG YLS | IPG YLS + custom SHG stage |

### §3.4 Radiometric power

### §4.4 Battery Sizing — Eclipse duration correction

LRO data shows worst-case eclipse up to 120–124 hr (not 72 hr as previously stated). Added dual-mode eclipse survival:

| Mode | Load | Conditions |
|---|---|---|
| Full survival | ~1 kW | Short eclipses (≤72 hr) |
| Hibernation | ~500 W | Extended eclipses (>72 hr): battery heat + avionics keep-alive only |

Key insight: at 500 W (hibernation), 72 kWh usable / 500 W = 144 hr coverage, exceeding the 124 hr worst case. Battery stays at 325 kg — no upsizing needed.

## Phase 2: New Section — Lunar Surface Environment (§7.4)

Added after §7.3 Long-Term Structural Durability. Two sub-sections:

**§7.4.1 Landing Plume and Dust**
- Ballistic trajectories in vacuum; no lingering cloud; settles in seconds post-shutdown
- All sensitive surfaces stowed during landing, deployed after
- No aeolian transport post-deployment (no atmosphere)
- Electrostatic levitation: low flux at 50 m elevation; anti-static coatings prudent
- Micrometeorite secondary ejecta: low flux over 10-year mission; not mission-level concern

**§7.4.2 Radiation Environment**
- GCR + SPE, no magnetosphere
- Starship hull ~4 mm SS provides 5–10 g/cm² shielding for internal electronics
- Sunday-only limits cumulative dose (~416 hr/yr vs. 8,760 continuous)
- Rad-hard avionics baseline
- Open item: SHIELDOSE analysis needed for SPE worst case
